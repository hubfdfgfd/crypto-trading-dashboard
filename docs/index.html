Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Crypto Trading Dashboard ("the App") is a cryptocurrency market data visualization tool. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.

Network Usage

The App connects to CoinGecko's public API to fetch live cryptocurrency market data (BTC prices). Premium users may also connect to a custom server for enhanced indicators. No personal information is sent in these requests.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. Premium subscribers do not see ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat - For managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data through Google Play Billing. See their Privacy Policy.
  • CoinGecko API - For fetching live cryptocurrency market data. See their Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your Android device settings. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Google Play Store. Purchase and subscription data is managed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store payment information.

Data Storage

The App does not store any data persistently. All displayed data is fetched in real-time and discarded when the App is closed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
