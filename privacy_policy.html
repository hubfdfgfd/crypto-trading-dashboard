Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Crypto Trading Dashboard ("the App") is a cryptocurrency market data visualization tool. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.

Network Usage

The App connects to a user-configured server to fetch cryptocurrency market indicators (price, RSI, MACD, Williams %R). No personal information is sent in these requests. If no server is configured, the App displays demo data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

The App does not store any data persistently. All displayed data is fetched in real-time and discarded when the App is closed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
