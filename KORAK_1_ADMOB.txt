=================================================================
  KORAK 1 - GOOGLE ADMOB REGISTRACIJA
=================================================================

1. Otvori u browseru:
   https://admob.google.com

2. Klikni "GET STARTED" ili "SIGN IN"
   - Koristi isti Google nalog kao Play Console

3. Klikni "Apps" → "ADD APP"
   
4. Popuni:
   ✓ Platform: ANDROID
   ✓ App name: Crypto Trading Dashboard
   ✓ Is your app listed on Google Play?: NO (jos nije)
   ✓ Klikni "CONTINUE"

5. Kreiraj BANNER AD UNIT:
   ──────────────────────
   - Klikni "Ad units" → "GET STARTED"
   - Select format: BANNER
   - Ad unit name: Dashboard Banner
   - Klikni "CREATE AD UNIT"
   
   → KOPIRAJ "Ad unit ID" (izgleda: ca-app-pub-XXXX/YYYY)
   
6. Kreiraj INTERSTITIAL AD UNIT:
   ────────────────────────────────
   - Klikni "Ad units" → "ADD AD UNIT"
   - Select format: INTERSTITIAL
   - Ad unit name: Dashboard Interstitial
   - Klikni "CREATE AD UNIT"
   
   → KOPIRAJ "Ad unit ID" (izgleda: ca-app-pub-XXXX/YYYY)

7. Dodaj APP ID:
   ──────────────
   - Idi na "App settings"
   - Kopiraj "App ID" (izgleda: ca-app-pub-XXXX~YYYY)
